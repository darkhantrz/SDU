Leadership Team

Dr. Davronzhon Gaipov, PhD

Rector

Davronzhon Gaipov is the Rector of Suleyman Demirel University. He is the head of the University executive team. He oversees operational, academic, research and internationalization activities at the University, including services in support of academic pursuits, e.g.: student affairs, library services and IT.

Dr. Andrey Bogdanchikov, PhD

Vice Rector for Academic Affairs

Andrey Bogdanchikov- Vice Rector of Аcademic Аffairs at University Suleyman Demirel. His duties include coordinating and providing academic support to schools, faculties, including the library.

Dr. Ali Kochak, PhD

Vice Rector for Administrative Affairs

Ali Kochak is Vice Rector of Administrative Affairs at Suleyman Demirel University. He is responsible for administrating and coordinating the works of the human resource department, marketing department, student recruitment, automation, IT support, legislation, maintenance department, and the continuous education center. Further, Ali Kochak is responsible for the infrastructural development of the University.

Dr. Sanat Kozhakhmet, PhD

Vice Rector for Scientific Research

Sanat Kozhakhmet- Vice-Rector of the University named after Suleyman Demirel on scientific research. His duties include coordinating scientific processes and providing support to schools, faculties, as well as scientific departments of the university.

Mr. Yerhanat Huandah, MA

Vice Rector for Financial Affairs

Yerhanat Huandah – Vice Rector of Financial Affairs at Suleyman Demirel University. He is the head of the University Board of Trustees and has broad powers to manage business processes. He is responsible for strategic development, public relations with government agencies and other organizations. The Vice Rector oversees the financial and fundrasing activities associated with the University.

Mr. Malik Satayev, MA

Vice Rector for Social Development

Malik Satayev is Vice Rector for Social Development at Suleyman Demirel University. He is responsible for administrating and coordinating the works of the social affairs department, department of career and corporate development, accomodation department. He is also the Head of the Board of discipline and Head of the Advisors Board. Malik Satayev is responsible for the discipline and educational, cultural-mass work of the University.

Mr. Erkin Shaimerdenov

Head of Rector’s Office

Erkin Shaimerdenov is the Head of Rector’s Office. He advises a wide range of special projects, initiatives and executive responsibilities, including university-wide long-term planning processes. Erkin Shaimerdenov is also the secretary of the Board of Trustees.
